Lacrosse shoes are designed for the specific demands of the sport — lateral agility on natural grass and turf, ankle support during quick direction changes, and cleat configurations that provide grip without restricting speed.

Top options in 2024 include the Nike Vapor Edge, Under Armour Highlight, and New Balance Freeze v3. When choosing, prioritize fit, cleat type (molded vs. detachable), and whether the shoe is rated for both field and turf surfaces.
